Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

How long does express delivery take?

Express delivery typically takes 1-2 business days for domestic shipments. Same-day delivery is available for local shipments placed before 12 PM.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 100 countries worldwide. Delivery times vary by destination, typically 3-10 business days.

How can I track my shipment?

You can track your shipment using the tracking number provided via email. Real-time updates are available on our website and mobile app.

What items are prohibited?

We do not ship hazardous materials, firearms, illegal substances, perishable items without proper packaging, and items that violate local laws.
